What are these? GPTs are custom made ChatGPT bots that are designed for a specific task. These GPTs are made to help you get along in a foreign language. They’re primarily translators but they will also correct your grammar, explain the translations, suggest more appropriate idioms. They show their workings so you can check that what’s come out is really what you mean.

How do I use them? Click to add them to your ChatGPT then just... chat to them. Anything you include in “quotes” will be translated directly - you can use this instead of Google Translate or DeepL. You can also just chat with them for more general language help.
